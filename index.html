About Us

Founded in Fall 2017, the University of Maryland (UMD) AI Club is a student-led organization dedicated to artificial intelligence. Our mission is to provide all UMD students with the tools and resources they need to immerse themselves with AI. We do this by:

Maintaing a nurturing and inclusive environment

Hosting workshops and other public events

Engaging in collaborative projects and programming

Self-Driving Car

Self-Driving Car
Using supervised learning, we are building a self-driving car. We bought a model car, and fitted it with sensors and an Adafruit (which will contain the Robot Operating System, or ROS). We will then drive it around manually; along the way, it will understand suitable driving circumstances, and hopefully drive itself accordingly. For this project, we are working with members of the Self-Driving Car Club.
